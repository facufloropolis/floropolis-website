# How to Run Profitable Flower Arranging Workshops

Flower arranging workshops are one of the highest-margin revenue streams available to florists. A 2-hour workshop for 12 people can generate $600-1,500 in revenue from $100-250 in materials. That's 60-85% margin — better than any retail arrangement or wedding you'll ever sell.

And the benefits go beyond the workshop itself: every attendee becomes a potential retail client, a social media advocate, and a word-of-mouth referral engine.

## The Business Case

### Workshop Economics

| Metric | Budget Class | Standard | Premium |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Price per person | $55-65 | $75-95 | $120-150 |
| Attendees | 10-12 | 8-12 | 6-10 |
| Revenue | $550-780 | $600-1,140 | $720-1,500 |
| Materials per person | $12-18 | $18-28 | $30-45 |
| Total materials | $120-216 | $144-336 | $180-450 |
| Venue cost | $0-100 | $0-150 | $0-200 |
| Beverages/snacks | $20-40 | $30-60 | $50-100 |
| **Gross profit** | **$274-524** | **$270-744** | **$270-950** |
| **Margin** | **50-67%** | **45-65%** | **38-63%** |
| Time investment | 4-5 hours total | 4-5 hours total | 5-6 hours total |

**The sweet spot:** A standard workshop at $85/person with 10 attendees = $850 revenue, $250 in costs = $600 gross profit in 4-5 hours. That's $120-150/hour for your time.

## Workshop Formats

### Public Workshops (Open Registration)

Anyone can sign up. Marketed on Instagram, Eventbrite, your website.

**Best for:** Building your brand, growing your email list, attracting new clients
**Pricing:** $55-120/person
**Frequency:** Monthly or bi-monthly
**Size:** 8-15 people

### Private Group Workshops

A company, friend group, or organization books you for a private session.

**Best for:** Higher revenue per event, corporate relationships, team building market
**Pricing:** $65-150/person (or flat fee $500-1,500 for the group)
**Frequency:** On-demand
**Size:** 6-25 people

### Corporate Team Building

Companies book you for a team bonding activity.

**Best for:** Highest per-person pricing, corporate account leads, B2B relationships
**Pricing:** $95-175/person
**Frequency:** On-demand
**Size:** 10-30 people

### Bridal Shower / Bachelorette Workshops

The bride's party comes for a flower arranging class.

**Best for:** Wedding lead generation, premium pricing, Instagram content
**Pricing:** $85-135/person
**Frequency:** On-demand (seasonal peak March-September)
**Size:** 6-15 people

## Class Structure

### The 2-Hour Template (Most Popular)

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 0:00-0:15 | Welcome, introductions, drinks | People arrive, settle in |
| 0:15-0:30 | Flower overview + design principles | Teach 3-4 basic concepts |
| 0:30-0:45 | Demonstration | You build an arrangement live, explaining each step |
| 0:45-1:30 | Hands-on arrangement time | Walk around, help, adjust, teach |
| 1:30-1:45 | Finishing touches + care tips | How to keep their arrangement fresh |
| 1:45-2:00 | Photo time + wrap up | Group photo, individual arrangement photos, hand out cards |

### What to Teach (Keep It Simple)

Beginners only need 4 concepts:
1. **Start with greenery** — Create the shape/framework first
2. **Add focal flowers** — Largest blooms go in first at different heights
3. **Fill with secondary flowers** — Medium blooms between the focals
4. **Add texture and finish** — Filler, final greenery, adjust

**Don't overcomplicate it.** The goal is for everyone to leave with an arrangement they're proud of, not to become a professional florist in 2 hours.

## Marketing Your Workshops

### Instagram (Primary Channel)

- **Announce 3-4 weeks before:** Post the date, theme, price, and registration link
- **Countdown Stories:** 1 week, 3 days, 1 day before
- **During the class:** Post Stories of students working, arrangements in progress
- **After:** Share the best arrangements (with student tags). This is your best content
- **Testimonials:** Ask 2-3 students for a short video testimonial after class

### Eventbrite / Registration

Use Eventbrite (free for free events, 6% fee for paid) or a simple Google Form + Venmo/PayPal for payment.

**Registration page must include:**
- Date, time, location
- What's included (all materials, vase to take home, refreshments)
- What they'll make (photo of example arrangement)
- Price + booking link
- "No experience necessary" (reduces hesitation)

### Pricing Psychology

| Strategy | Implementation |
|---------|---------------|
| Early bird | $10 off for bookings 2+ weeks in advance |
| Bring a friend | $10 off each when booking 2+ |
| Group pricing | Book 6+ at $10 off each (for private groups) |
| Gift option | "Gift a workshop" — sell as experience gift (holiday goldmine) |

## Scaling Workshops

| Stage | Monthly Revenue | What Changes |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 workshop/month | $600-1,000 | You do everything |
| 2-3 workshops/month | $1,200-3,000 | Standardize supplies, streamline setup |
| 4+ workshops/month | $2,400-6,000+ | Hire an assistant, consider private bookings only |
| Workshop brand | $5,000+/month | Corporate program, franchise model, online class |
