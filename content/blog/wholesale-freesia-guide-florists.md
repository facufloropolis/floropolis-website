## Care and Handling

### At Receiving
1. **Unbox promptly** — freesia are ethylene sensitive
2. **Cut stems at 45 degrees** — 1 inch minimum
3. **Remove lower foliage** — standard practice
4. **Cool water with preservative** — room-temp water initially, then cool storage
5. **Store at 36-40°F** — standard cooler
6. **Keep upright** — stems are delicate and bend easily

### Key Care Points

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Sequential blooming** | Buds open bottom-to-top over 5-10 days. This is a feature, not a flaw |
| **Remove spent blooms** | As lower flowers fade, gently remove them. Upper buds keep opening |
| **Fragile stems** | Freesia stems snap easily. Handle gently, support in arrangements |
| **Ethylene** | Very sensitive. Keep away from fruit, vehicle exhaust, decaying flowers |
| **Water** | Light drinkers. Don't need deep water |
| **Light** | Phototropic — bend toward light. Rotate arrangements |

### Common Problems

| Problem | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Buds not opening | Harvested too tight, cold storage too long | Warm room (70°F) for 24 hours |
| Stems snapping | Too brittle, mishandled | Support with other stems in arrangement, handle carefully |
| Quick wilting | Ethylene or bacterial water | Fresh cut, clean water, remove from ethylene |
| Fragrance fading | Normal as flowers age | Supplement with fresh stems |

## Design Applications

### Fragrance Marketing

Freesia's biggest asset is its scent. Use it strategically:
- **Place freesia near your shop entrance** — the fragrance draws people in
- **"Fragrance bouquet" product line** — market arrangements specifically for their scent: freesia + stock + garden roses + sweet peas
- **Add 3-5 freesia to any arrangement** for a fragrance bonus. Tell the client: "I added freesia for the beautiful scent"
- **Sympathy note:** Some people find strong fragrance overwhelming during grief. Ask before adding freesia to sympathy orders

### Wedding
- **Bridal bouquet accent:** Freesia add delicate color and incredible scent
