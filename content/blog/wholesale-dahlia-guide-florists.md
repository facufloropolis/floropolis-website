# Wholesale Dahlias for Florists: Varieties, Season, and Sourcing Guide

Dahlias are the flower of the moment. They dominate wedding Pinterest boards, they're the crown jewel of fall arrangements, and clients request them by name. A single dinner plate dahlia can be the focal of a $75 arrangement.

But dahlias are also one of the most challenging flowers to source and handle. Short season. Fragile. Expensive. This guide covers how to source, price, and work with dahlias profitably during their limited window.

## The Dahlia Season Problem (and Opportunity)

Dahlias have the shortest season of any popular wedding flower:

| Month | Availability | Quality | Price |
|-------|-------------|---------|-------|
| January-May | NOT AVAILABLE | — | — |
| June | Very limited (early season) | Variable | Highest ($4-6/stem) |
| July | Increasing | Good | High ($3-5/stem) |
| August | Peak | Excellent | Moderate ($2-4/stem) |
| September | Peak | Excellent | Best value ($1.50-3.50/stem) |
| October | Decreasing | Good-excellent | Moderate ($2-4/stem) |
| November | Very limited (late season) | Variable | High ($3-5/stem) |
| December | NOT AVAILABLE | — | — |

**The opportunity:** Because supply is limited, clients are willing to pay premium prices for dahlias. A dinner plate dahlia that costs $3-5 wholesale can anchor a $75-100 arrangement.

## Care and Handling

Dahlias are the most perishable popular flower. Handle with extreme care.

### At Receiving
1. **Open immediately.** Dahlias deteriorate rapidly in closed boxes
2. **Cut stems** at 45 degrees, 1-2 inches
3. **Remove ALL lower foliage** — dahlia leaves rot quickly
4. **Place in cool water** (not warm) with flower food
5. **Store at 36-40°F** — dahlias prefer cold
6. **Do NOT stack.** Petals bruise easily. Give each stem space

### Key Care Points
- **Vase life is short:** 4-7 days max, even farm-fresh. Design and deliver within 24-48 hours of receiving
- **Petals are fragile.** Handle by the stem only. Never touch the bloom
- **Ethylene sensitive.** Keep far from fruit, exhaust, and other ethylene sources
- **Heavy heads.** Dinner plate dahlias are top-heavy. Use mechanics (foam, chicken wire) to support them in arrangements
- **Wilting heads.** If a head wilts, it won't recover. Remove and replace. This is why you order 10-15% extra buffer

### Expected Vase Life

| Source | Vase Life |
|--------|----------|
| Farm-direct / local farm | 5-7 days |
| Wholesale market | 3-5 days |
| Imported (rare) | 2-4 days |

**Pro tip:** Source dahlias from local farms when possible. Dahlias are one of the few flowers where local sourcing provides genuinely better quality than imports, because they're so perishable.

## Off-Season Alternatives

When dahlias aren't available (November-June), suggest these alternatives:

| Alternative | Season | Why It Works |
|------------|--------|-------------|
| **Garden roses** | Year-round | Similar lush, layered petals |
| **Ranunculus** | Dec-May | Layered, romantic, fills the same niche |
| **Chrysanthemum (spider/disbud)** | Year-round | Similar impact at lower cost |
| **Protea** | Year-round (imported) | Statement focal, dramatic |
| **Carnation (Nobbio)** | Year-round | Ruffled petals, surprisingly dahlia-like |
