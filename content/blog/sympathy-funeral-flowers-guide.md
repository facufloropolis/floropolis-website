# Sympathy and Funeral Flowers: A Complete Guide

Sympathy flowers are one of the most meaningful gestures during a time of loss. For families, knowing what to send and when can feel overwhelming. For florists, sympathy work is a significant and consistent revenue stream that requires sensitivity and skill.

This guide covers both perspectives: what families need to know about ordering sympathy flowers, and what florists need to know about producing and selling them profitably.

## Types of Sympathy Flower Arrangements

### For the Service

| Arrangement | Description | Price Range | Who Sends |
|-------------|------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Standing spray** | Large arrangement on an easel, displayed at the service | $150-400 | Close family, employers, organizations |
| **Casket spray** | Arrangement placed on top of the closed casket | $200-600 | Immediate family (usually ordered by funeral home) |
| **Wreath** | Circular arrangement on a stand | $100-300 | Family, friends, organizations |
| **Cross/heart shape** | Sympathy tribute in a specific shape | $150-350 | Close family members |
| **Urn arrangement** | Arrangement surrounding the urn (cremation services) | $75-200 | Family |

### For the Home

| Arrangement | Description | Price Range | Who Sends |
|-------------|------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Vase arrangement** | Standard flower arrangement in a vase | $50-150 | Friends, coworkers, acquaintances |
| **Basket arrangement** | Flowers in a basket (no vase needed) | $50-120 | General sympathy |
| **Plant** | Peace lily, orchid, or succulent garden | $30-100 | Anyone (lasts longer than cut flowers) |
| **Fruit/gourmet basket** | Food basket instead of flowers | $40-120 | When flowers aren't appropriate or preferred |

## Best Flowers for Sympathy

### Traditional Choices

| Flower | Color | Meaning | Best For |
|--------|-------|---------|---------|
| **White lilies** | White | Purity, innocence, restored soul | Universal sympathy standard |
| **White roses** | White | Reverence, respect, new beginnings | Standing sprays, casket pieces |
| **White chrysanthemums** | White | Grief, loyalty (many cultures) | European/Asian funerals |
| **Carnations** | White, red, pink | Love, remembrance | Tributes, sprays, budget-friendly |
| **Gladiolus** | Various | Strength of character | Tall arrangements, sprays |
| **Hydrangeas** | White, blue, purple | Understanding, gratitude | Vase arrangements, home delivery |

## Etiquette: When and What to Send

### Timing

| When to Send | What to Send | Where to Send |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Day of death** (when you hear the news) | Call or text the family. Flowers can wait | — |
| **1-3 days after** (before the service) | Standing spray, wreath, or arrangement for the service | Funeral home |
| **Day of service** | If you didn't send before, bring an arrangement | Funeral home or venue |
| **1-2 weeks after** | Vase arrangement or plant for the home | Family's home |
| **1-6 months after** | Flowers on the anniversary, birthday, or a random Tuesday | Family's home |

**The most meaningful flowers often arrive 2-4 weeks after the funeral.** Everyone sends flowers the first week. By week 3-4, the family feels alone. A surprise delivery says "I'm still thinking of you."

### What to Write on the Card

| Relationship | Message Examples |
|-------------|----------------|
| Close friend/family | "We love you and are here for whatever you need. [Name]" |
| Colleague/professional | "With deepest sympathy from the [Company] team" |
| Acquaintance | "Thinking of you during this difficult time. With sympathy, [Name]" |
| When you didn't know the deceased | "I'm sorry for your loss. [Deceased's name] was loved by many. [Name]" |

**Keep it simple.** Don't try to explain the loss or offer religious comfort unless you know the family's beliefs.

### Cultural Considerations

| Culture/Religion | Flower Customs | Notes |
|-----------------|---------------|-------|
| **Christian** | White flowers traditional. Standing sprays at service | Flowers welcome at service and home |
| **Catholic** | White flowers, crosses, rosary beads | Flowers at service, sometimes at gravesite |
| **Jewish** | Simple, modest arrangements. Often prefer donations | Flowers typically NOT sent to the service. May be sent to home after shiva |
| **Muslim** | Simple, modest. Often prefer charitable donations | Some traditions discourage flowers. Check with family |
| **Buddhist** | White and yellow flowers. Chrysanthemums common | Flowers at service, offerings at altar |
| **Hindu** | White flowers, marigold garlands | Flowers at service. No beef-derived products near arrangements |
| **Secular/non-religious** | Any appropriate arrangement | Most flexible. Follow the family's personality |

## For Florists: Sympathy as a Business

### Revenue Potential

Sympathy work is the most consistent, least seasonal revenue stream for a flower shop:

| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|-------------|
| % of total revenue | 15-25% for retail shops |
| Average order value | $85-150 |
| Seasonal variation | Minimal (death doesn't follow a calendar) |
| Repeat rate | High (funeral homes refer repeatedly) |
| Competition | Local florists + online services (FTD, 1-800-Flowers) |

### Building Funeral Home Relationships

Funeral homes are the #1 referral source for sympathy flowers. Build these relationships:

1. **Visit every funeral home** within 15 miles. Introduce yourself. Leave a card and a sample arrangement
2. **Be reliable.** When a funeral home calls, say yes. Deliver on time, every time
3. **Offer a trade discount.** 10-15% off for funeral home referrals. They appreciate it and refer more
4. **Keep a "sympathy menu" card** at the funeral home front desk. Families pick from your arrangements
5. **Deliver to the funeral home** (not just the family). This is where the service arrangements go

### Sympathy Product Line

Build a standard sympathy menu with 3-4 price points:

| Product | Wholesale Cost | Retail | Margin |
|---------|---------------|--------|--------|
| "Comfort" basket (budget) | $18-25 | $65-75 | 63-72% |
| "Grace" vase arrangement | $22-30 | $85-100 | 70-74% |
| "Peace" standing spray | $40-60 | $150-200 | 67-73% |
| "Honor" premium spray/casket | $60-90 | $250-400 | 72-76% |

### Efficiency Tips

- **Batch produce.** When you have 3 sympathy orders in a day, design them together
- **Standardize.** Have a go-to sympathy design that you can produce quickly. White roses + white lilies + eucalyptus = classic, fast, always appropriate
- **Stock sympathy staples.** White roses, white lilies, white carnations, eucalyptus, ruscus, leather leaf. Always in your cooler
- **Same-day capability.** Sympathy orders are often urgent. Be able to produce and deliver within 4-6 hours
