# Wholesale Sunflowers for Florists: Varieties, Pricing, and Design Guide

Sunflowers are one of the most requested flowers in retail floristry. Customers love them. They're affordable to source, impressive in size, and carry a universally positive emotional association. For florists, they're a high-margin staple.

This guide covers what florists need to know about sourcing wholesale sunflowers: varieties, pricing, seasonality, care, and design applications.

## Why Sunflowers Sell

- **Consumer recognition:** Everyone knows what a sunflower is. No education needed
- **Emotional response:** Sunflowers are associated with happiness, optimism, and summer. They make people smile
- **Size impact:** A single sunflower stem has more visual impact than 3 stems of most other flowers
- **Price perception:** Customers perceive sunflower arrangements as generous and abundant
- **Year-round demand:** Peak in summer, but steady demand throughout the year
- **Long vase life:** 7-12 days with proper care (farm-direct)

### Pollen-Free Varieties (Essential for Commercial Work)

For professional use, always specify **pollen-free** varieties. Pollen stains clothing, tablecloths, and skin. Most modern commercial sunflower varieties are bred pollen-free:

- ProCut series — pollen-free
- Sunrich series — pollen-free
- Vincent's Choice — pollen-free

**If a client asks for "natural" sunflowers with pollen,** warn them about staining. Provide a care card with this information.

**Best ordering strategy:**
- **Summer (June-September):** Order domestic. Best quality, lowest prices. Feature prominently
- **Fall (October-November):** Still good availability. Fall color varieties (Moulin Rouge, Ring of Fire) sell especially well
- **Winter-Spring:** Import-dependent. Higher cost. Use as accent, not focal, to manage costs

## Care and Handling

### At Receiving

1. **Unpack immediately.** Sunflowers are phototropic (they turn toward light). Extended box time causes bent stems
2. **Cut stems** 2 inches at a 45-degree angle with sharp shears
3. **Remove lower leaves** — only leave 2-3 leaves at the top near the bloom
4. **Place in clean water** with flower food. Use room temperature water
5. **Store upright** in a cooler at 36-40°F (2-4°C). Sunflowers wilt in heat faster than most flowers

### Common Problems

| Problem | Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Drooping heads | Dehydration, too warm | Fresh cut, cold water, cooler storage |
| Bent stems | Phototropism in box | Store upright immediately. Wrap loosely with paper if needed |
| Premature petal drop | Ethylene exposure, old stock | Check freshness, keep away from fruit, use flower food |
| Brown center | Natural aging | Normal after day 7-8. Remove petals for "center only" design look |
| Slimy stems | Bacteria in water | Change water daily, add bleach drop, recut stems |

## Design Applications

### Summer Arrangements
- **Sunflower + eucalyptus:** The classic combination. Fresh, cheerful, modern
- **Sunflower + white roses + ruscus:** Elegant summer arrangement
- **All-sunflower bouquet:** 10-15 stems in a Mason jar or ceramic vase. Simple, impactful
- **Sunflower + wildflower mix:** Daisies, snapdragons, solidago. Garden-style

### Fall Arrangements
- **Moulin Rouge sunflower + orange roses + autumn greens:** Deep, warm tones
- **Sunflower + chrysanthemum + wheat:** Harvest theme
- **Sunflower + berries + dried elements:** Textural fall centerpiece

### Wedding Applications
- **Bridal bouquet:** 3-5 mini sunflowers + white roses + eucalyptus (rustic/country weddings)
- **Centerpieces:** 3 sunflowers in a low vessel with greenery (affordable impact)
- **Aisle markers:** Single sunflower in a jar per row (charming, budget-friendly)
- **Photo backdrop:** Mass arrangement of 50-100 sunflowers (Instagram-worthy)

### Corporate and Event
- **Reception desk:** Single large sunflower in bud vase. Cheerful, affordable, replaced weekly
- **Conference table:** 3 sunflowers in a low arrangement. Bright without obstructing sightlines
- **Summer event:** Sunflower centerpieces are the most affordable large-impact event flower

## Sourcing Tips

### Order Specifications

When ordering wholesale sunflowers, specify:
- **Variety name** (not just "sunflowers")
- **Stem length** (minimum 24" for arrangements)
- **Bloom size** (minimum 4" for centerpieces, 5"+ for focal)
- **Pollen-free** (always for commercial/event work)
- **Color** (classic yellow vs. specialty colors)
- **Bunch size** (typically 5 or 10 stems per bunch)

### Quality Indicators

**Good quality:**
- Petals firm and upright (not drooping)
- Center tight and compact (not fuzzy or open)
- Stem firm and straight
- Leaves green and turgid
- No brown spots on petals

**Reject if:**
- Petals already curling back
- Center is fuzzy/releasing pollen (past prime)
- Stem is soft or bendable
- Brown spots or yellowing leaves
- Strong musty smell
