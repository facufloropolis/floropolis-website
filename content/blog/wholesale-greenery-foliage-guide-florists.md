## Care and Handling

### General Greenery Care

1. **Unpack and hydrate immediately** — greenery is more forgiving than flowers but still needs prompt hydration
2. **Strip lower leaves** — remove anything that would be below the waterline
3. **Cut stems** at an angle, 1-2 inches off
4. **Room temperature water** with flower food
5. **Store cool** (36-40°F) in clean buckets

### Variety-Specific Tips

| Variety | Special Care | Warning |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Eucalyptus** | Releases oils — change water when it gets murky. Don't mix with sensitive flowers (ranunculus) in storage | Oils can clog sensitive flower stems |
| **Ruscus** | Nearly indestructible. Can go 3 weeks in water | None — easiest greenery to handle |
| **Salal** | Trim brown leaf edges. Mist occasionally | Can develop brown spots in high humidity |
| **Ferns** | Mist daily. Keep humid. Dry environments kill them fast | Very sensitive to ethylene and dry air |
| **Aspidistra** | Wipe leaves with damp cloth. Can be scored/folded for design | Splitting at the top if dehydrated |
| **Smilax** | Use immediately. Doesn't store well. Keep in water until setup | Very perishable — 5-7 day max |
| **Monstera** | Wipe clean. Heavy — secure in arrangements | Will topple lightweight vessels |
