# Wholesale Flower Availability by Season: The Florist's Year-Round Sourcing Calendar

One of the most common questions florists face — from clients and from themselves — is "what's in season right now?" Knowing what's available (and what isn't) is the difference between profitable arrangements and expensive substitutions.

This guide maps wholesale flower availability month by month, so you always know what to order, when to order it, and what to tell clients who ask for peonies in November.

## The Year at a Glance

### Year-Round Availability (Always in Season)

These flowers are available every month from Ecuador and Colombia. They're the foundation of any florist's regular ordering.

| Flower | Wholesale Price | Vase Life | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-----------|-------|
| **Roses** (all varieties) | $0.85-1.10/stem | 12-14 days | 40+ varieties always available from Ecuador |
| **Eucalyptus** (Baby Blue, Silver Dollar) | $0.65/stem | 14+ days | Most versatile green in floristry |
| **Ruscus** (Italian, Israeli) | $0.60/stem | 14+ days | Structure green, holds up in any climate |
| **Gypsophilia** (Baby's Breath) | $0.45/stem | 10-14 days | Classic filler, year-round supply |
| **Bird of Paradise** | $2.50/stem | 14+ days | Tropical, always available from Ecuador |
| **Heliconia** (Bihai, Rostrata) | $2.50/stem | 14-21 days | Longest vase life in wholesale |
| **Anthurium** (Red, Pink, White, Green) | $1.80/stem | 14+ days | Year-round tropical from Ecuador |
| **Delphinium** | $0.94/stem | 8-10 days | Available year-round, peaks spring |

**Why Ecuador matters:** Because Ecuador sits on the equator with consistent 12-hour days year-round, many flowers that are "seasonal" in temperate climates are grown year-round in Ecuador. This is the farm-direct advantage — access to varieties when your local market says they're "out of season."

## Month-by-Month Availability

### January
**Peak season for:** Anemones, ranunculus (starting), roses
**Coming into season:** Spring stems ramping up
**Going out:** Nothing — fresh start to the year

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses (all varieties) | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Peak — order freely |
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Starting — quality improving weekly |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Available |
| Eucalyptus | $0.65 | Year-round |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |

**January tip:** Start building your Valentine's Day inventory. Red roses sell out at the farm level 4-6 weeks before V-Day.

### February
**Peak season for:** Anemones, ranunculus, roses (Valentine's spike)
**Warning:** Valentine's Day (Feb 14) = highest prices of the year for red roses

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses | $0.94-1.50+ (V-Day premium) | Price spike week of Feb 7-14 |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Peak season |
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Peak season |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Available |
| Tulips (Dutch import) | Market price | Seasonal import |

**February tip:** Order V-Day roses by January 5th for guaranteed allocation. By late January, premium varieties are spoken for.

### March
**Peak season for:** Ranunculus (absolute peak), anemone, delphinium, all spring stems
**Best month for:** Variety and selection. More flowers available in March than any other month.

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | PEAK — best quality of the year |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Peak, season ending in ~6 weeks |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Strong availability |
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Normal pricing (post-V-Day) |
| All greens | $0.45-0.65 | Year-round |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |

**March tip:** Stock up on ranunculus and anemones now. These are the highest-demand spring stems, and availability narrows through April-May.

### April
**Peak season for:** Ranunculus (ending), delphinium, roses, spring greenery
**Coming into season:** Peonies (late April, limited)
**Planning for:** Mother's Day (May) — order roses NOW

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Available but declining |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Late season — limited |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Strong |
| Peonies | Market price | Starting — limited, premium |
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Normal |

**April tip:** Lock in your Mother's Day rose order by April 14 (4 weeks out). Ecuador farms allocate early.

### May
**Peak season for:** Peonies (if you can get them), roses, delphinium
**Ending:** Ranunculus, anemone
**Holiday:** Mother's Day (2nd Sunday) — second biggest flower holiday

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Peonies | Market price ($3-8/stem) | Peak but supply is limited |
| Roses | $0.94-1.20 (M-Day premium) | Price bump around Mother's Day |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Available |
| Ranunculus | Ending | Last orders of the season |
| Anemone | Done | Season over |

**May tip:** Peonies are the most requested flower in May. If you can't source them, garden roses are the best substitute — similar look, more reliable supply, lower cost.

### June
**Peak season for:** Roses, delphinium, summer greens
**Summer transition:** Spring stems ending, summer varieties starting
**Wedding season:** Peak — order 2-3 weeks ahead

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses (all) | $0.85-1.10 | Normal pricing, high demand |
| Delphinium | $0.94 | Available |
| Sunflowers | Market price | Starting |
| Hydrangea | Market price | Starting (Colombian) |
| All greens | $0.45-0.65 | Year-round |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |

**June tip:** Wedding season peaks June-October. Build standing orders for your predictable weekly needs and supplement with event-specific orders.

### July
**Peak season for:** Roses, sunflowers, tropicals
**Full summer:** Focus on heat-resistant varieties

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Sunflowers | Market price | Peak |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round — strong summer demand |
| Hydrangea | Market price | Available |
| Dahlias | Market price | Starting (local growers) |

**July tip:** Tropical arrangements sell well in summer. Heliconias and anthuriums are year-round from Ecuador and offer 14-21 day vase life — perfect for July heat.

### August
**Peak season for:** Dahlias (local), sunflowers, roses, tropicals
**Transition month:** Summer to fall

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Dahlias | Market price | Peak from local growers |
| Sunflowers | Market price | Available |
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |
| Early fall greens | Market price | Starting |

**August tip:** Start planning your fall inventory. Warm-toned roses (terracotta, burnt orange, dusty rose) sell well September-November.

### September
**Peak season for:** Dahlias (peak), roses, chrysanthemums, fall greens
**Wedding season:** Still strong
**Fall transition:** Warm tones in high demand

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Dahlias | Market price | Peak — best quality |
| Chrysanthemums | Market price | Starting |
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |
| Fall foliage | Market price | Starting |

### October
**Peak season for:** Chrysanthemums, fall roses, autumn greens
**Ending:** Dahlias, sunflowers
**Coming back:** Ranunculus, anemone (late October)

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Chrysanthemums | Market price | Peak |
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Starting — early season |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Starting |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |

**October tip:** Ranunculus returns in October-November. Early-season stems are smaller but quality improves weekly through January.

### November
**Peak season for:** Holiday greens, chrysanthemums, roses
**Holiday prep:** Thanksgiving, then Christmas ordering begins
**Back in season:** Ranunculus, anemone

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 | Year-round |
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Building — good availability |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Available |
| Holiday greens | Market price | Peak |
| Chrysanthemums | Market price | Available |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |

**November tip:** Place your Christmas rose order by mid-November. Like Valentine's and Mother's Day, farm allocation starts early.

### December
**Peak season for:** Ranunculus, anemone, roses, holiday greens
**Holiday:** Christmas/Hanukkah/New Year's
**Best month for:** Starting spring stem orders

| Available | Wholesale | Status |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Roses | $0.85-1.10 (slight holiday premium) | High demand |
| Ranunculus | $1.74 | Strong — great quality |
| Anemone | $1.30 | Peak starting |
| Holiday greens | Market price | Peak |
| Tropicals | $1.80-2.50 | Year-round |
| Poinsettias | Market price | Seasonal staple |

**December tip:** December-May is the best 6 months for flower variety and quality. Spring stems are ramping up, roses are consistent, and tropicals are always available. Plan your biggest events for this window.
