# Wholesale Chrysanthemums for Florists: Varieties, Pricing, and Profit Guide

Chrysanthemums are the workhorse of the wholesale flower industry. They last longer than almost any other cut flower (14-21 days), come in more shapes and colors than most florists realize, and offer some of the best margins in floristry.

Yet many florists underuse chrysanthemums, limiting them to filler or funeral work. This guide covers how to source, price, and use mums profitably across every category of floral design.

## Why Chrysanthemums Deserve More Respect

- **Longest vase life** of any common cut flower: 14-21 days (farm-direct)
- **Year-round availability** from South American farms
- **Extreme variety:** From tiny button poms to dramatic 6-inch spider mums
- **Lowest waste rate:** Less shrinkage = higher profit per stem purchased
- **Cultural significance:** Top flower in Japan, essential for Día de los Muertos, and the #1 selling cut flower globally
- **Price stability:** Less price volatility than roses, peonies, or other premium flowers

| **Spring** | Daisy mums, green cushions | Mixed spring arrangements | Easter, Mother's Day |
| **Summer** | Spider mums, bright colors | Tropical/bold designs | Summer events |
| **Fall** | Bronze, rust, burgundy, orange | Autumn arrangements, Thanksgiving | Halloween, Día de los Muertos, Thanksgiving |
| **Winter** | White disbuds, green spiders | Holiday elegance, New Year | Christmas, New Year's |

### Fall Is Mum Season

September through November is prime chrysanthemum selling time:
- **Thanksgiving centerpieces:** Bronze + yellow + orange mums are the classic Thanksgiving flower
- **Día de los Muertos (Nov 1-2):** Marigolds AND yellow/orange chrysanthemums. Major sales opportunity in Hispanic markets
- **Fall retail bunches:** Mixed spray mums in autumn tones. Inexpensive to produce, strong demand
- **Football mums (Homecoming):** Football-sized mums with ribbons. $15-40 retail for a $3-5 wholesale flower. Best single-item margin in floristry

## Design Applications

### Everyday Retail

- **Mixed bouquet filler:** 2-3 spray mum stems add volume to any $35-55 arrangement at minimal cost
- **Monochromatic mum arrangement:** All-white cushion mums = modern, elegant, affordable. All-green = trendy
- **Grab-and-go bunches:** 5 spray mum stems wrapped = $10-15 retail from $3-4 wholesale

### Funeral and Sympathy

Chrysanthemums are traditionally appropriate for funerals across most cultures:
- **Standing spray:** White football mums as focal, white spray mums as filler
- **Casket spray:** Disbud mums provide coverage at lower cost than all-roses
- **Sympathy arrangement:** White cushion mums + white roses + greenery

### Wedding and Events

Modern wedding design is embracing chrysanthemums:
- **Green spider mums** in modern/minimalist weddings
- **White cushion mums** as a hydrangea alternative (similar volume, lower cost, longer life)
- **Colored spray mums** in bohemian/wildflower-style weddings
- **Mass chrysanthemum installation:** 200 stems of white spider mums creates a stunning photo backdrop for $200-300 in wholesale

### Corporate

- **Weekly lobby arrangement:** Green or white spray mums + eucalyptus = professional, lasts all week
- **Conference table:** Low arrangement of cushion mums = clean, long-lasting

## Care and Handling

### At Receiving
1. Cut stems at 45-degree angle (remove bottom 2 inches)
2. Remove ALL foliage below waterline — chrysanthemum leaves decompose quickly
3. Place in room temperature water with flower food
4. Store at 36-38°F (2-3°C) — mums tolerate cold well

### Key Care Points
- **Change water every 3-4 days** (they can go longer than most flowers)
- **Remove wilting blooms** on spray types to keep the arrangement looking fresh
- **Ethylene resistant:** Mums are among the most ethylene-resistant flowers — safe near fruit
- **No misting needed** (unlike hydrangeas) — they're low maintenance
