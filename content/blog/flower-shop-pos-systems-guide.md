# Flower Shop POS Systems: Best Options for Florists in 2026

Your POS system is the operational backbone of your flower shop. It handles sales, inventory, customer data, delivery routing, and reporting. The wrong system costs you hours per week in workarounds. The right one saves you time and actually helps you sell more.

This guide compares the best POS options for florists in 2026 — from general-purpose systems to florist-specific software.

## What Flower Shops Need (That Other Retail Doesn't)

Before comparing systems, understand what makes floral POS different:

| Feature | Why It Matters for Florists |
|---------|---------------------------|
| **Delivery management** | 30-60% of orders need delivery. You need routing, driver tracking, time windows |
| **Wire service integration** | If you do FTD/Teleflora/Lovingly, POS must integrate or you're double-entering |
| **Perishable inventory** | Flowers expire. Your POS should track freshness/waste, not just quantity |
| **Custom orders** | Every arrangement is different. You need flexible pricing, not rigid SKUs |
| **Occasion-based CRM** | Track birthdays, anniversaries, recipients — not just purchase history |
| **Photo-based selling** | Customers choose by look, not SKU. Your system needs visual order entry |
| **Seasonal demand** | Valentine's Day and Mother's Day traffic is 5-10× normal. System must handle spikes |

## Decision Framework

### For Startup Shops (Under $10K/month revenue)

**Recommendation: Square (free plan) + Google Sheets for delivery tracking**

Why: Zero upfront cost, start selling immediately, upgrade when you need more. Don't spend $100+/month on a POS when you're still building your customer base.

| Cost | Amount |
|------|--------|
| POS software | $0 |
| Card reader | $0-149 |
| Monthly total | $0-60 |

### For Established Shops ($10-40K/month)

**Recommendation: Floranext ($99/mo plan) or Square Plus ($60/mo) + delivery app**

Why: You need delivery management and customer tracking. Floranext gives you florist-specific features. Square Plus is simpler but cheaper.

| Cost | Amount |
|------|--------|
| POS software | $60-99/mo |
| Hardware | $149-799 |
| Monthly total | $60-99 |

| Cost | Amount |

| Monthly total | $99-199 |

## Making the Switch

If you're switching POS systems:

1. **Export your customer data** before switching. Download everything: names, emails, phone numbers, order history
2. **Overlap systems for 2 weeks.** Run both simultaneously during a slow period (NOT during Valentine's or Mother's Day)
3. **Train your team before switchover.** Minimum 3-4 hours of hands-on practice per person
4. **Start with basic features.** Get comfortable with sales processing before activating delivery routing, CRM, etc.
5. **Never switch during a holiday.** January or July are ideal switch months

## What Actually Matters

After helping hundreds of florists evaluate POS systems, the most important factors are:

1. **Speed of sale.** How fast can you ring up a custom order? If it takes more than 60 seconds, it's too slow
2. **Delivery management.** If 30%+ of your orders deliver, you need delivery tracking built in (or bolted on)
3. **Customer data.** Can you see past orders, preferences, and recipient info at a glance during a phone call?
4. **Reliability.** Does it work when your internet goes down? (offline mode is essential)
5. **Cost vs. revenue.** A $100/mo POS that saves 5 hours/week of admin is worth it ($20/hr = $400/mo saved)
